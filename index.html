How to Play

Welcome to the Gem Game! Your goal is to collect as many gems as possible within the given time limit. Here are the rules:

  • Click on the gems that appear on the screen to collect them.
  • Each gem has a different point value:
    • Amber: 10 points
    • Amethyst: 50 points
    • Diamond: 20 points
    • Emerald: 25 points
    • Sapphire: 30 points
    • Stone: -30 points
  • Avoid clicking on stones as they will reduce your score.
  • The game ends when the time runs out.
